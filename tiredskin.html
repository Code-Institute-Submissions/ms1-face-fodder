FACE MASK FOR TIRED SKIN

this mask has anti-aging, antioxidant, exfoliating and rejuvenating properties
INGREDIENTS
  • 1 tbsp Ground Coffee Beans
  • 1 tbsp Cocoa Powder
  • 2 tbsp Natural Yogurt
  • 1/2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1/2 tbsp Honey
METHOD

Caffeine has potent anti oxidant properties and protects your skin cells from environmental pollutants and UV damage, slowing down the process of ageing. I've put olive oil into the mix as well because it has superb moisturising properties that will help revitalise your skin by replenishing lost natural oils.

Simply mix all the ingredients together in a bowl and VOILA!! Apply to clean skin on your face and leave for 30 mins. Rinse off completely with a damp cloth.

Storage: Maybe refrigerated for 1 week.
