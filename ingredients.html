FOOD YOUR SKIN WILL LOVE

GINGER

Ginger has benefitial properties that help your skin flush out toxins. This magic food ingredients encourages blood circulation through your skin, as well as help reduce the damage caused by free radicals.

Beauty benefits: Anit-againg, rejuvinating, reduce spot flare ups

AVOCADO

Avocados contain a bunch of compounds called carotenoids which are antioxidants and help soak up all those skin-damaging free radicals to protect your skin. They contain vitamins A, C D & E powerful antioxidants that protect against free radical damage from everyday UV light exposure.

A rich source of omega-3 fatty acids (similar to those found in fish oil) avocados are also high in sterolin which has shown to soften skin and help prevent age spots. Avocado oil can be useful for healing sun-damaged skin.

Beauty benefits: Moisturising, healing, anti-againg

COFFEE

Caffeine has potent anti oxidant properties and protects your skin cells from environmental pollutants and UV damage, slowing down the process of ageing. It has been shown to increase circulation to the skin by helping the new cells flourish!

Beauty benefits: Regenerating, increase blood circulation
